Initializing and Setting Up Your New Trezór Wallet

This is where the magic begins—your wallet is born here.

Steps:

  • Open Trezór Suite and select “Create new wallet”

  • Choose “Standard Wallet” or “Hidden Wallet” for extra layers of protection

Setting a PIN: Your First Defense Against Unauthorized Access

Your PIN is your wallet’s front door lock. Without it, anyone with physical access could drain your funds.

To set your PIN:

  • Go to Settings > Device > Security in Trezór Suite

Choose a PIN that’s unique, not your birthday or "1234." Keep it secret, keep it safe.
